    About Alzheimer's

    What is Alzheimer's?

    Alzheimer's is a progressive neurodegenerative disorder that primarily affects older adults. It is characterized by memory loss, cognitive decline, and behavioral changes. The risk of developing Alzheimer's increases with age.

    Age of Onset

    Alzheimer's typically begins after the age of 65, but early-onset Alzheimer's can occur in individuals as young as their 40s or 50s.

    Impact and Care

    Alzheimer's patients require significant care and support as the disease progresses. Memory loss can lead to confusion and disorientation, and individuals may become lost or forget their surroundings.

    How AlzHelper Can Help

    AlzHelper is designed to assist individuals with Alzheimer's by providing support and reminders. Its features include:

    2.Safe zone.
    3.Strong emotions tracking.






    How was AlzHelper made?

    AlzHelper is made with some electrical components which are :

    Arduino UNO: Arduino uno is a microcontroller board based on ATmega328P(datasheet). It has 14 digital I/O ports (6 of which can be used as PWM outputs), 6 analog inputs, a 16MHz ceramic resonator (CSTCE16M0V53-R0), a USB connection, a power socket, an ICSP header and a reset button. Contains everything needed to support the microcontroller; Simply connect it to a computer with a USB cable or power it with an AC to DC adapter or battery to get started.It is the brain of AlzHelper.

    GPS NEO 6M: The NEO-6M GPS module is a well-performing complete GPS receiver with a built-in 25 x 25 x 4mm ceramic antenna, providing powerful satellite search capability. Using the power and signal indicators, you can monitor the status of the unit.It provides accurate location information for AlzHelper.

    GSR Sensor: GSR stands for galvanic skin response, which is a way to measure the electrical conductivity of the skin. Strong emotion can stimulate the sympathetic nervous system, causing the sweat glands to produce more sweat. Grove - GSR allows you to detect these powerful feelings by attaching two electrodes to two fingers on one hand, this helps in monitoring emotional states.

    SIM800L: Small GSM/GPRS module SIM800L is a mini cellular module that allows GPRS transmission to send and receive SMS and make and receive voice calls. Low cost, small size and quad-band support make this unit an ideal solution for any project requiring long-range communication, enabling communication for live tracking and notifications.
